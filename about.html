About us

Glogg Mind Care Centres is a brain child of a team of mental health practitioners, who work with the government under the ministry of health. This is after majority of the professionals had observed that most of the companies were experiencing low employee productivity due to unattended psychological issues that eventually complicate to become mental illnesses. In response to this, the professionals started offering probonal services to improve the employees’ wellness and the company productivity.

This went on for 3 years. As the demand for the services increased, the team decided to register the services as a Private limited company under the name Glogg Mind Care Centers in October 2017 under the company’s Act 2015. The main aim being to create sustainability for the services.

Mission

To provide affordable, accessible and professional mental health care services to employees and the public, facilitate training, research and participate in mental health policy

Vission

To be an international mental health care facility the preferred for employees and public well being
